## q1

- \(y = \cos^{-1}(\sqrt x)\)
  - \(u = \sqrt{x}\), \(v = \cos{^-1}(u)\)
- \(f'(x) = \frac{1}{-\sqrt{1 - u^2}} * \frac{1}{2}x^{-\frac{1}{2}}\)
- \(f'(x) = \frac{1}{-\sqrt{1 - x}} * \frac{1}{2}x^{-\frac{1}{2}}\)

## q2

- \(y = \sin^{-1}(x^2)\)
  - \(u = x^2\), \(v = \sin^{-1}(u)\)
  - \(u' = 2x\), \(v' = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\)
- \(y' = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^4}}*2x\)

## q3

- \(y = (1 + \tan^{-1}(x))^3\)
  - \(u = x, v = 1 + \tan^{-1}(u), y= v^3\)
- \(y' = 3v^2 * (\frac{1}{1 + u^2}) * 1\)
- \(y' = 3(1 + \tan^{-1}(x))^2 * \frac{1}{1 + x^2}\)
