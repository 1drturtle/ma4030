## q1

- \(f(x) = x^2 - 3x - 4\)
- \(f'(x) = 2x - 3\)
  - \(0 = x^2 - 3x - 4\)
  - \(0 = (x - 4)(x +1)\)
  - \(x = 4\)
- a) \(f'(4) = 2(4) - 3 = 5\), 5 ft/s
  - \(11 = x^2 - 3x - 4\)
- b) 8.246 ft/s

## q2

- \(f(x) = 3x^3 - 7x\)
- \(f'(x) = 9x^2 - 7\)
- \(f''(x) = 18x\)
- a) \(f'(1) = 9(1)^2 - 7 = 2\) m/s
- b) \(f''(1) = 18(1) = 18\) m/s\(^2\)
- c)
  - \(0 = 9x^2 - 7\)
  - \(7 = 9x^2\)
  - \(x^2 = \frac{7}{9}\)
  - \(x = \sqrt{\frac{7}{9}}\)
  - \(f''(\sqrt{\frac{7}{9}}) = 18(\sqrt{\frac{7}{9}}) = 18( \sqrt{\frac{7}{9}}) = 15.87\) m/s\(^2\)

> \(f''(x) == \frac{d^2y}{dx^2}\)
